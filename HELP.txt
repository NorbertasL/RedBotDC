########## RedBot Help Info ##########
 "!redBot help" brings up the help info.
 "!timmers" shows the spawn time for bosses
 "!BOSS_NAME EXTRA_INFO" will post on boss channel that the boss is dead with the current time.
		BOSS_NAME = replace with boss name that just dies eg:zaken.
		EXTRA_INFO = replace with extra info, like if it droped something and what clan killed it.
		Example: !aq NODROP MostHated
		Result: AQ [DEAD] - 27/05 12:02 (UTC) NODROP MostHated
